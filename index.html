HW5 - LeetCode 18 4Sum

Veröffentlicht am 2017-10-22

Given an array S of n integers, are there elements a, b, c, and d in S such that a + b + c + d = target? Find all unique quadruplets in the array which gives the sum of target.

Note: The solution set must not contain duplicate quadruplets.

这道题最开始想用 DFS，不过答案传递比较麻烦，BFS 也比较难控制。分治法是比较好的解决方案。在给出的 nums 中，先排序，方便之后去重；然后依次循环分解成 3Sum、2Sum 问题解决。

HW3 - LeetCode 11 Container With Most Water

Veröffentlicht am 2017-10-08

Given n non-negative integers a1, a2, …, an, where each represents a point at coordinate (i, ai). n vertical lines are drawn such that the two endpoints of line i is at (i, ai) and (i, 0). Find two lines, which together with x-axis forms a container, such that the container contains the most water.

Note: You may not slant the container and n is at least 2.

这道题的难点在于「宽度」和「高度」之间的取舍。如果单纯排列组合比较大小，时间复杂度为 O(n^2)，性能较差。
想要提升比较的效率，就需要尽可能的降低取舍的维度。如果从两边开始向中间靠拢，则为了补偿牺牲的「宽度」，一定要更高的「高度」，这样的条件过滤就会让比较次数少许多。
